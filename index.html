What is W/AV?

Hi-fi standalone streaming hardware that bridges the gap between digital music and analog control. It transforms your music streaming experience into a local, tangible device, allowing you to interact with your music through physical knobs, sliders, and buttons while it lives in your home.

It's your digital collection, made personal, playable, and proudly displayable—just like vinyl, but built for now.

Direct Control

Custom-built analog controls that let you physically engage with your music.

Visual Feedback

Real-time waveform visualizations that draw you deeper into every beat and detail.

Seamless Integration

Connects effortlessly to your existing streaming accounts—no phone needed.
